General Questions

What types of keyboards do you offer?

We offer a wide range of keyboards, including mechanical, ergonomic, wireless, and customizable keyboards.

What are the benefits of mechanical keyboards?

Mechanical keyboards offer several benefits, such as durability, customizable key switches for different typing preferences, tactile feedback for a better typing experience, and overall improved performance.

Can I customize my keyboard?

Yes, you can customize your keyboard with various options such as keycaps, key switches, RGB lighting, and other customizable features to suit your preferences.

Do you offer keyboard accessories?

Yes, we offer a range of keyboard accessories including wrist rests, keycap pullers, switch testers, and cleaning kits to enhance your keyboard experience.

What is NKRO and why is it important?

NKRO (N-Key Rollover) allows multiple keys to be pressed simultaneously without any input conflicts or key ghosting, making it crucial for gaming and fast typing tasks.

How do I clean and maintain my keyboard?

To clean your keyboard, use a keycap puller to remove keycaps and then gently clean the keyboard with a soft brush or compressed air. Avoid using liquid cleaners directly on the keyboard.
